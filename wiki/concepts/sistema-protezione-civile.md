# Sistema Protezione Civile (Civil Protection System)

## Definition

The Italian civil protection system is a coordinated national and regional framework for preventing, preparing for, and responding to natural and human-made disasters. Governed by D. Lgs 2 gennaio 2018, n. 1, it distributes responsibilities across central government, regional authorities, and local administrations.

## Structure

- **National level**: Dipartimento della protezione civile (Civil Protection Department)
- **Regional level**: Regione Lombardia manages its own protection civil system
- **Local levels**: Provincial and municipal civil protection structures
- **Functional centers**: Centri Funzionali Decentrati for specialized risk monitoring

## Key functions

- Risk forecasting and monitoring ([[allertamento]])
- Emergency response coordination
- Public communication and alert dissemination
- Interagency coordination (meteorological services, hydrological networks, etc.)
