# Allertamento (Alert/Alerting)

## Definition

Allertamento refers to the formal process of issuing warnings and alerting civilian protection structures about imminent or ongoing natural hazards. It is a coordinated activity managed through hierarchical channels from national to municipal levels, governed by standardized risk classifications and communication protocols.

## Examples

- [[decreto-15960-approvazione-procedure-cfmr]]: Formalizes alerting procedures for CFMR operations
- Regional alerts issued for hydraulic, hydrogeological, and meteorological risks
- Criticality notices transmitted through GESTCOM system

## Key aspects

- Managed at national level by Dipartimento della protezione civile and regional level by CFMR
- Uses standardized message formats and risk phase classifications (white, yellow, orange, red)
- Integrated with public alarm system IT-Alert (introduced by directive of October 23, 2020)
- Coordinated across regional, provincial, and municipal protection civil structures
