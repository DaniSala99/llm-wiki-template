**Ingested**: 2026-05-12
**Type**: Operational Manual

## Summary

Operational manual detailing the daily technical evaluation activities conducted by CFMR operators for assessing natural risks and issuing civil protection alerts in Regione Lombardia. Covers assessment of hydrogeological, hydraulic, meteorological, wind, ice formation, and avalanche risks using standardized color code classifications and multiple data sources.

## Key claims

- CFMR operators conduct daily risk evaluations using meteorological bulletins from ARPA Lombardia and consultation with forecasters
- Risk assessment integrates historical situation analysis, meteorological modeling (ECMWF, COSMO, MOLOCH), and monitoring systems (IRIS, Sinergie, FEWS)
- Color codes (P1-P4 with variations by risk type) classify hazard levels based on danger predictions
- Operators interface with ARPA Meteo forecasters, Regional Operations Room staff, and ARIA Spa technical support
- Briefing meetings occur daily at 12:00 with mandatory CFMR operator attendance during elevated risk forecasts
- Risk evaluations must be coordinated with neighboring regions sharing Po basin boundaries
