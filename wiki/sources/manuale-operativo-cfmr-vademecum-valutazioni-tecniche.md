## Summary

Daily operational procedures for [[centro-funzionale-monitoraggio-rischi]] operators conducting technical evaluations of natural risks, prerequisite to issuing Regione Lombardia civil protection alerts. Defines operator roles, meteorological consultation procedures, and detailed risk assessment workflows for each hazard type.

## Distribution

- ARIA Spa operational personnel (Sala Operativa)
- Regione Lombardia - UO Protezione Civile (Centro Funzionale Rischi Naturali e Sistema di allertamento)

## Key Operational Procedures

### 1. Operator Roles and Interfaces

CFMR operators coordinate with:
- **Previsore ARPA Meteo** — meteorological forecaster (reachable 338.1861504)
- **Funzionario CFMR e PO CFMR** — CFMR officials and procedure officer
- **Dirigente Struttura Gestione Emergenze** — Emergency management director
- **Dirigente Reperibile** — On-call director (covers weekends and 18:00-08:00 hours)
- **Personale Tecnico Sala Operativa Regionale** — Regional operations room technical staff
- **Assistenza Tecnica ARIA Spa** — Technical support provider

### 2. Meteorological Consultation

**Tools Consulted for Situational Analysis**:
- Ghost meteorological models (ECMWF, COSMO 5M, COSMO 2I, FWI index)
- FEWS hydraulic models
- Sinergie hydraulic node (Nodo Idraulico di Milano)
- IRIS monitoring system ([[arpa-lombardia]])
- Sinergie system
- Aegis AIPO
- Radar meteorologico
- Sistema Rilevamento Fulmini (Lightning detection system)
- Piogge Equivalenti method analysis

**ARPA Vigilance Bulletins**:
- Received by 11:30 daily from ARPA Lombardia
- Operators phone ARPA meteorological forecaster for clarification on:
  - Validity periods (decorrenze)
  - Peak event timing (fase acuta)
  - Affected elevations
  - Event evolution
- Daily briefing participation: 12:00 briefing with ARPA Meteo (weekdays)

**Briefing Activation Rules**:
Expanded multi-operator briefing required when vigilance bulletins contain:
- [[rischio-idrogeologico]], [[rischio-idraulico]], wind risk — from code P2 and above
- [[rischio-temporali]], snow risk — from P2 and above
- Fire risk (IB) — from code P3 and above
- [[rischio-valanghe]] — from code A and above

### 3. Technical Support Activation

- ARIA Spa technical support notified of potential alert issuance to activate IT support infrastructure

### 4. Color Code Proposal

Detailed methodology in manual operativo "TRACCIAMENTO E TRASMISSIONE DELLA VALUTAZIONE"

### 5. Regional Operations Room Updates

**Weekend and Holiday Procedure**:
- Phone Regional Operations Room (02.67657100) to share:
  - Collected evaluations and indications
  - Specific operational guidance for system updates
  - Vigilance/pre-alert/alert phase activation
  - Large landslide emergency plan communications (PE Frane)

**Continuous Updates**:
- Operations room may be consulted anytime during evaluation
- Provides territorial reports (e.g., fire reports from firefighters - IB da VVF AIB CURNO)
- Offers specialized technical support

### 6. Risk Assessment Workflows

#### Hydrogeological Risk (Rischio Idrogeologico)

**Daily Procedure**:
1. **Previous day assessment**: Review ground effects, territory status (infrastructure, roads, transport) via:
   - Monitoring systems (IRIS ARPA Lombardia, Sinergie, Aegis AIPO)
   - Operations room log
   - Active alert documents
   - Active large landslide emergency plans

2. **Current day assessment**: Verify forecast effects and territory status via:
   - Numerical meteorological models (ECMWF, COSMO, MOLOCH)
   - Monitoring systems (IRIS, Sinergie, Radar, Lightning detection)
   - Equivalent precipitation method analysis
   - Operations room log
   - Active emergency plans
   - Consultation with territorial surveillance teams

3. **ARPA bulletin verification**: Receive and verify danger predictions in:
   - ARPA Vigilance Bulletins
   - Adverse meteorological condition notices
   - Danger codes for 6-12-24h rainfall per homogeneous zone

4. **Forecaster consultation**: Direct discussion with meteorological forecaster during 12:00 daily briefing for:
   - Greater details on predictions
   - Event evolution
   - Timing, forecast confidence level
   - Precipitation distribution

5. **Inter-regional coordination**: Verify and compare with neighboring regions on Po basin boundary zones for alert code homogenization

#### Hydraulic Risk (Rischio Idraulico)

**Differs from hydrogeological primarily in monitoring systems**:
- Water level readings (livelli idrometrici)
- Dam and embankment conditions (rotture arginali)
- Bank erosion (erosioni spondali)
- Hydraulic-hydrological modeling (FEWS, Nodo Idraulico Milano, stochastic models, EFAS)

#### Strong Thunderstorms (Temporali Forti)

- Monitoring systems: CAE system addition
- Previous and current day effects verification
- Coordination with borders

#### Snow (Neve) and [[rischio-valanghe]] (Avalanche)

- Seasonal assessment procedures
- Coordination with avalanche monitoring services

#### Fire Risk (Incendi Boschivi)

- AIB (Antincendi Boschivi) vigilance monitoring
- Dry condition indices
- Vegetation monitoring

#### Wind Risk (Vento Forte)

- Wind speed models and thresholds
- Impact on transport infrastructure

## Homogeneous Zones (Zone Omogenee)

Risk evaluations organized by homogeneous meteorological and hydrological zones within Regione Lombardia territory.
