## Purpose

Administrative compliance and regularit review procedures for [[centro-funzionale-monitoraggio-rischi]] operational activities and formal approval processes.

## Scope

Reviews compliance of CFMR procedures and operations with:
- Regional administrative requirements
- Delegation of authority protocols
- Documentation and record-keeping standards
- Approval workflows
- Budget and resource constraints
