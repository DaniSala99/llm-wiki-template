# Legge n. 30 del 16 Marzo 2017 — Delega Riforma Protezione Civile

**Source**: `raw/NORMATIVA PROTEZIONE CIVILE/Legge_n.30_16-marzo-2017_GU_delega_riforma-protezione-civile.pdf`
**Ingested**: 2026-05-12
**Type**: Delegation Law
**Purpose**: Authorizes government reform of civil protection system

## Summary

Delegation law empowering the Italian government to enact comprehensive reforms of the civil protection system. This delegation formed the basis for subsequent issuance of [[decreto-legislativo-2-gennaio-2018]] (D. Lgs 2/2018) which consolidated all civil protection regulations into a unified code.

## Authority Granted

Authorization for government to:
- Reorganize civil protection system structures
- Consolidate fragmented civil protection legislation
- Establish unified operational frameworks
- Define regional-national coordination mechanisms

## Implementation

Led to creation of **Codice della Protezione Civile** (D. Lgs 2 gennaio 2018, n. 1)
