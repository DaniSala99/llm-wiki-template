**Type**: Regional Decree (Italian)

## Summary

Decreto issued by Direzione Generale Sicurezza e Protezione Civile on October 18, 2023, approving formal procedures for the Centro Funzionale Monitoraggio Rischi (CFMR) in preparing, approving, and issuing civil protection alert documents. This decree establishes standardized operational procedures following an audit conducted by Fondazione CIMA in 2021-2022 that identified opportunities to strengthen procedural efficiency and operational practices within Regione Lombardia's CFMR.

## Key claims

- CFMR is responsible for risk monitoring, forecasting, and surveillance activities within Regione Lombardia's civil protection system
- The decree approves seven detailed operational manuals as integral parts of the procedures
- Annual verification of procedure contents is mandated with updates only for documents requiring modification
- Alert activity is regulated under D. Lgs 2 gennaio 2018, n. 1 (Italian Civil Protection Code)
- The Central Functional Decentrated System manages alerts for hydraulic, hydrogeological, meteorological, and avalanche risks
- All involved personnel must be kept informed and have access to the approved procedures
