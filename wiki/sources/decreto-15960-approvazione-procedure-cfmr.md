## Summary

Decreto issued by the Direzione Generale Sicurezza e Protezione Civile (Dirigente Andrea Zaccone) formally approving standardized procedures for the [[centro-funzionale-monitoraggio-rischi]] (CFMR) in preparing, approving, and issuing civil protection alert documents. The decree is based on an audit conducted by Fondazione CIMA during 2021-2022 that identified opportunities to strengthen procedural efficiency.

## Legal Foundation

- **D. Lgs 2 gennaio 2018, n. 1** ("Codice della protezione civile") — establishes that regions manage civil protection systems and alert activities
- **DPCM 27 febbraio 2004** — national alert system operational directives for hydraulic and hydrogeological risks
- **DPCM 3 dicembre 2008** — emergency management directives recognizing strategic role of regional civil protection
- **DGR n. XI/4114** (21 December 2020) — regional update to alert system directive
- **LR n. 27** (29 December 2021) — Regione Lombardia new civil protection regulations

## Key Decrees and Directives Referenced

- **8 febbraio 2013**: Po River basin coordination for flood management [[rischio-idraulico]]
- **8 luglio 2014**: Operations for major dam areas
- **12 agosto 2019**: National regional alert system for [[rischio-valanghe]] (avalanche risk)
- **23 ottobre 2020**: Civil protection alert and IT-Alert public alarm system (modified 7 February 2023)
- **10 febbraio 2016**: Standardization of meteorological-hydraulic risk alert messages and operational phases

## Regulatory History (Regione Lombardia)

- **7 marzo 2005** — DPR n. 3408: Activated regional Decentrato Centre; assigned U.O. Protezione Civile responsibility for issuing regional criticality notices
- **8 ottobre 2014** — DPR n. 9262: Assigned identified managers (per reperibilità prospetto) authority to issue regional criticality notices
- **23/11/2021** — DGR n. XI/5584: Commissioned audit with Fondazione CIMA
- **28/03/2022** — DGR n. XI/6200: Audit continuation approval

## Approved Procedures and Manuals

The decree approves 7 operational manuals as integral parts:

1. **VADEMECUM VALUTAZIONI TECNICHE OPERATORE CFMR** — Technical evaluation procedures
2. **TRACCIAMENTO E TRASMISSIONE DELLA VALUTAZIONE** — Tracking and transmission workflows
3. **Check list_Allertamento CFMR** — Alert issuance checklist
4. **Check list_Dati di Monitoraggio ed Applicativi** — Data systems monitoring checklist
5. **COMPILAZIONE ED AGGIORNAMENTO GESTCOM** — GESTCOM application manual
6. **TESTI PREDEFINITI** — Pre-defined message templates
7. **COMPILAZIONE ED AGGIORNAMENTO DEWETRA - WEB ALERT** — DEWETRA-WebAlert manual

## CFMR Functions Covered by Audit

The Fondazione CIMA audit examined:
- **Previsione** (Forecasting)
- **Monitoraggio** (Monitoring)
- **Sorveglianza** (Surveillance)

## Key Decision Points

1. **Annual verification** of procedure content required with updates only when necessary
2. **No financial burden** — states the decree introduces no new costs to Regione Lombardia
3. **Personnel requirement** — all staff must be informed and have access to approved procedures
4. **Strategic alignment** — supports Objective 2.5.4 "Strengthen regional civil protection system" from XII Legislative Term Regional Development Program (approved 20 June 2023)
