# Decreto Legislativo 2 Gennaio 2018, n. 1 — Codice della Protezione Civile

**Source**: `raw/NORMATIVA PROTEZIONE CIVILE/DECRETO LEGISLATIVO 2 gennaio 2018.docx`
**Ingested**: 2026-05-12
**Type**: Primary Legislative Code
**Official Name**: D. Lgs 2/2018 - Codice della Protezione Civile

## Summary

The foundational Italian legislative code governing the civil protection system. Establishes organizational structures, defines roles and responsibilities at national, regional, and local levels, and sets procedures for all civil protection activities including forecasting, prevention, emergency management, and recovery.

## Key Scope

**Article 11, Comma 1**: Establishes that Regions exercise legislative and administrative authority to discipline civil protection system organization within their territories, including forecasting, prevention, and alert activities.

**Article 17**: Assigns national government (Dipartimento della Protezione Civile) and regional/provincial governments governance and management of alert systems, utilizing:
- Centri Funzionali Decentrati (Decentrated Functional Centers) for hydraulic, hydrogeological, and adverse meteorological risks
- National meteorological services
- Monitoring and surveillance networks
- Centers of competence (Article 21)
