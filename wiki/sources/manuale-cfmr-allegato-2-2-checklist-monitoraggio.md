**Ingested**: 2026-05-12
**Type**: System Monitoring Checklist

## Purpose

Verification checklist for [[sala-operativa-regionale]] (SOR) operators confirming operational monitoring data systems and application availability during alert operations.

## Monitoring Systems Verified

- IRIS ARPA Lombardia (monitoring system)
- Sinergie (hydraulic node)
- FEWS (hydraulic modeling)
- Ghost meteorological models
- Lightning detection systems
- Radar meteorological
- ARPA vigilance bulletin feeds
- AVVISi and alert notification channels

## Applications Verified

- AllertaLOM (APP and WebAPP)
- GESTCOM (alert management)
- DEWETRA-WebAlert (national dissemination)
- Operations Room Log (Registro di Sala)
- Email systems
- Alert archive systems

## Scope

Applied by SOR operators daily to confirm all systems operational before and during alert operations.
