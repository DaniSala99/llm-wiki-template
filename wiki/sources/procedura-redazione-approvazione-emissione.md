# Procedura: Redazione, Approvazione ed Emissione Documenti di Allertamento di Protezione Civile

**Source**: `raw/2023.10.18_Decreto n. 15960 - Approvazione PROCEDURE CFMR/PROCEDURA Redazione, Approvazione ed Emissione Documenti di Allertamento di Protezione Civile.pdf`
**Ingested**: 2026-05-12
**Type**: Master Procedure Document
**Scope**: Core workflow for Regione Lombardia civil protection alerts

## Overview

Master procedure governing complete workflow for preparing, approving, and issuing civil protection alert documents from initial risk evaluation through distribution to emergency management structures at regional, provincial, and municipal levels.

## Document Structure

This procedure establishes:
1. **Roles and responsibilities** — operators, officials, directors, technical staff
2. **Workflow stages** — evaluation → proposal → approval → issuance → dissemination
3. **Quality gates** — review points and sign-offs required
4. **System integration** — coordination across GESTCOM, DEWETRA, AllertaLOM, Operations Log
5. **Reference manuals** — links to detailed operational procedures

## Approval Authority

Approvers designated per [[decreto-del-presidente-regione-n-9262-2014]] (October 8, 2014) — assigned leaders within the reperibilità prospetto of the General Direction competent in civil protection.

## Key Governance

- **Annual verification** of procedure contents required
- **Updated versions** approved only when modifications necessary
- **Personnel access** — all involved staff must be informed and have access to procedures
- **No financial burden** — implementation requires no new regional spending
