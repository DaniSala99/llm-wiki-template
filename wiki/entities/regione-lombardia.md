# Regione Lombardia

**Type**: Regional Government (Place/Organization)

## Overview

Regione Lombardia is the regional government of Lombardy in northern Italy. Within civil protection contexts, it maintains a decentralized central functional system (Centro Funzionale Decentrato) responsible for monitoring, forecasting, and alert management for natural hazards affecting the region.

## Timeline

- 2005: Decreto Presidente Regione Lombardia n. 3408 activated the regional Decentrato Centre and assigned to Protection Civil Unit the task of issuing regional criticality notices
- 2014: Decreto Presidente Regione Lombardia n. 9262 formalized procedures for regional alert issuance
- 2020: DGR n. XI/4114 updated regional directive for alert management system
- 2021: Regional Law n. 27 ("Disposizioni regionali in materia di protezione civile") established new regional civil protection regulations
- 2023: Decreto n. 15960 approved formalized CFMR procedures
