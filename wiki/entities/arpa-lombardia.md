# ARPA Lombardia

**Type**: Regional Environmental Protection Agency

## Overview

ARPA Lombardia (Agenzia Regionale per la Protezione dell'Ambiente) is Regione Lombardia's environmental protection agency responsible for meteorological forecasting, environmental monitoring, and issuing vigilance bulletins on natural hazards.

## Related functions

- Issues daily meteorological vigilance bulletins with danger code forecasts
- Operates meteorological forecasting service (ARPA Meteo)
- Manages monitoring systems (IRIS - Integrated Regional Information System)
- Provides briefing meetings to coordinate forecasts with CFMR operators
