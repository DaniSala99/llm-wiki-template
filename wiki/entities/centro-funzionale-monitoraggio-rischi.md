# Centro Funzionale Monitoraggio Rischi (CFMR)

**Type**: Organizational Unit / Functional Center

## Overview

The Centro Funzionale Monitoraggio Rischi is a specialized unit within Regione Lombardia's civil protection system responsible for risk monitoring, forecasting, and surveillance activities. It forms part of the Decentrated Central Functional System and operates under the direction of the Protection Civil Unit (U.O. Protezione Civile).

## Responsibilities

- Monitor and forecast natural hazards (hydraulic, hydrogeological, meteorological, avalanche)
- Issue criticality notices and alert documents to regional, provincial, and municipal protection civil structures
- Maintain surveillance networks and coordinate with national meteorological services
- Update and manage operational information systems (GESTCOM, DEWETRA)
- Provide technical assessments and risk evaluations

## Timeline

- 2005: Established by Decreto Presidente Regione Lombardia n. 3408
- 2021-2022: Subject to audit by Fondazione CIMA examining procedural and operational criticalities
- 2023: Formal procedures approved via Decreto n. 15960
