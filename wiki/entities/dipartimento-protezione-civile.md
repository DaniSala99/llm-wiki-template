# Dipartimento della Protezione Civile

**Type**: National Government Department

## Overview

The Italian Department of Civil Protection operates at the national level and is responsible for managing and governing the national civil protection alert system. Works with regional governments and decentrated functional centers to ensure coordinated risk management across Italy.

## Role

- Governs and manages national alert system
- Coordinates with regional protection civil systems
- Sets operational directives for functional centers
- Issues national-level guidance on alert procedures
