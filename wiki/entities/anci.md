# ANCI — Associazione Nazionale Comuni Italiani

**Type**: National Organization / Municipal Association

## Overview

ANCI (National Association of Italian Municipalities) represents the interests and concerns of Italian municipalities in civil protection system governance. Provides municipal perspective on legislation and implementation requirements.

## Role in Civil Protection

- Represents municipal civil protection authorities
- Analyzes legislation impacts on municipalities
- Advocates for municipal capacity and resource needs
- Coordinates inter-municipal civil protection activities
